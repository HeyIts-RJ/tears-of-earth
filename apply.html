INTERNSHIPS

Tears of the Earth organization offers a number of opportunities for those who believe that their small actions can result in a large difference in our environment. The opportunities offered by us are as follows:

  • Summer Internship Program (5th June – 5th August).
  • Winter Internship Program (10th December -10th February).
  • Long-term Internship Program (3 months – 6 months).
  • Social Media /Web Development/Content Writing Internship Program (Rolling process).

Summer/Winter Internship Program (5th June/10thDecember – 5th August/10th February)*:

Summer Internship Program also known as Moral And Innovative Environmental Leadership Program comprises of events such as :

  • Surveys: We conduct various surveys to collect statistical information about environmental activities such as number of plastic bags manufactured as well as the air quality index of the air of particular localities, which gives us a rough idea that how much aware the people are about the current situation of the environment.
  • Leadership & Management Activities: The interns will be assigned various teams and events, all to their own-self to test their managerial and leadership skills.
  • Exploring inner self: At TOE we , not only help our interns help the environment but , we also try to explore their inner self by motivating them to showcase their hidden talents and perform activities which they have never ever done in public but has a potential to it, such as music,art,dance,lyrics writing etc.
  • Community Work & Documents: The interns will be invested with responsibility to perform some crucial documentation work of the organization and also various community works such as representing the organization at various important events at various institutes and organizations such as The American Centre and Russian Centre for Science and Culture.
  • Out-Station Events & Drives: Our organization provides opportunity for selected interns to accompany the team to various out station locations such as Madhya Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh etc, and perform various events and activities there such as Market Awareness Drives, Plantation Drives, and Village Awareness and Development Drives etc.
  • Volunteering: Interns are required to volunteer for various events and activities such as Nukkad nataks, strikes, awareness programs etc.
  • Environment Awareness Program :Tears Of the Earth Organization , organizes a one/two day awareness programs in various schools and colleges to aware and enlighten the students about the environment and tell them what role do they and their actions play on the environment.

*Students applying for winter internship can apply for either 1 or 2 month program as per their suitability.

Long-term Internship Program (3 months – 6 months):

Interns opting for the long term internship program are bound for at least 3 months and a maximum of 6 months of tenure in the organization, although the internship can be extended if the intern wishes to and the organization feels the need to .

Responsibilities and Work Culture: The interns opting this particular internship will be required to fulfil the following responsibilities:

  • Content Writing for various social media handles.
  • Managing feeds and traffic on various social media handles.
  • Providing support and maintenance for the organization’s official website.
  • Photo/Video editing for every particular event or those done in the past to post wherever required.
