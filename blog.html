Our Work

Causes and Fields of our Work

Climate Action-

Our organization even after countless failed attempts made by different organizations and individuals has not stopped trying to work for issues like climate change and climate crises. We indulge in different movements for nature as well as we conduct strikes for climate change.

Waste Segregation-

If we cannot stop the generation of waste, then why not we take care of what we generate? We at TOE try to aware as many people as possible on the topic of Waste Segregation. We conduct various nukkad nataks as well as awareness programs to aware the people about the importance of waste segregation.

Greenery and Water-

Tears of the Earth Organization has been conducting regular plantation drives since a very long time , in which we plant as well as we motivate people to plant more and more trees and not only just plant but to care for them too;

TOE also established various rain water harvesting plants in many localities of Delhi, from where the water was used to water the surrounding local parks and gardens.

Health & Society-

With today’s increasing levels of pollution, regular illness of the residents of our country is a pretty common thing. To keep a check on the people’s health, TOE with the collaboration of J.K.Foundation has been organizing free Health Checkups Camp as well as Blood Donation Camps every year since 2016.

Music & Art-

“All work and no play makes Johnny a dull boy”, and where awareness is concerned people always crave for a little entertainment along with the information, i.e. INFOTAINMENT. In order to provide that, people at TOE, compose and re-mix pre existing tracks in order to highlight the importance of the environment as well as ways to save it . We also conduct various Nukkad Nataks (Street Plays) and do sloganeering and poem chanting in order to aware the people.

Education & Awareness-

TOE conducts 1-2 day program in different schools and colleges, known as EAP-“Environment Awareness Program” where we tell the students their roles and importance in our environment and how can they create a difference.

Science & Tech-

We organize various workshops on the topics of environment and the upcoming technologies as well as the sustainability goals and how to achieve them. We train the young minds so that they can spread out our mission to their parents and from parents to the world.
